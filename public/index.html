ABOUT US

A Slice of Heaven on Earth

Nestled on the pristine shores of Pulau Redang, SalangSayang Resort has been a family-owned paradise since 1998. We offer 40 beautifully designed rooms across 8 unique types, each crafted to give you the ultimate island experience.

Our mission is to provide authentic Malay hospitality with modern comforts. Whether you're seeking adventure or relaxation, our dedicated team ensures every moment of your stay is unforgettable.
